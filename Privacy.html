Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

We collect the following Personal Data to optimize Your experience:

  • User Details: Email address, first name, last name, and phone number for account setup and communication.
  • Inventory Details: Product names, expiry dates, quantities, and nutritional information (via barcode scans or manual input).

Usage Data

Collected automatically during Your interaction with the Service, this includes:

  • Device details, such as IP address, browser type/version, and operating system.
  • Interaction logs, including:
    • Products added or removed from inventory.
    • Notifications generated for approaching expiry dates.
    • Real-time updates in shared household inventory.

Features Utilizing Data Collection

  • Inventory Management:Scan product barcodes and fill essential information like product name, expiry date, and quantity. Maintain a clear, visual inventory with expiry notifications to prioritize consumption.
  • Collaboration for Households: Enable shared inventory tracking with family or roommates. Real-time updates across multiple devices minimize waste and duplicate purchases.
  • Expiry Date Tracking and Notifications:Receive alerts when items near expiry. Suggest consumption ideas or donation options for nearing-expiry products.
  • Barcode Scanning and Manual Entry:Effortlessly add new inventory items via barcode scanning, which captures expiry dates and nutritional information automatically.
